Spaceship Inventory Management

A web application using Flask framework to manage inventory of a list of products in respective warehouses.

In this Demo a Spaceship wholesaler uses it to keep track of their inventory around various locations in the world.

  • Add/Edit/View Product
  • Add/Edit/View Location
  • Add/Edit/View ProductMovement
